# EDA Visualization Dashboard Summary

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Time Series Overview
- **Main Plot**: Demand over entire time period
- **Secondary Plots**: Rolling averages (7-day, 14-day)
- **Highlight**: Trend identification and anomalies

### 2. Seasonal Decomposition Grid
- **Trend Plot**: Long-term direction
- **Seasonal Plot**: Repeating patterns
- **Residual Plot**: Unexplained variance
- **Original Plot**: Full time series with components overlay

### 3. Pattern Analysis
- **Weekday Heatmap**: Demand by day of week across weeks
- **Monthly Distribution**: Box plot of demand by month
- **Year-over-Year**: Compare demand patterns across years

### 4. Statistical Plots
- **ACF Plot**: Autocorrelation for different lags
- **PACF Plot**: Partial autocorrelation analysis
- **Distribution Plot**: Histogram and KDE of demand

### 5. Relationship Analysis
- **Price vs Demand**: Scatter plot with trend line
- **Price Distribution**: Violin/box plots
- **Correlation Heatmap**: All variables correlation

## Visual Insights
1. Clear weekly seasonality visible in time series
2. Price inversely related to demand
3. Trend is relatively stable with cyclical variations
4. Few outliers but mostly consistent patterns
5. Month-to-month variation ~20-30% of mean demand
